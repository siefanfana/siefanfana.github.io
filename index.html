ARTIST BIO

Sierra Shih

Sierra is currently a student at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago pursuing her BFA with an emphasis in design. She is in the process of experimenting with different mediums and techniques as well as the content of her artwork. Previously having centered her artwork in social matters such as beauty standards, identity, growing up, and the concept of home, she worked primarily in 3D in order to create multimedia sculptures. She is now delving into the digital realm and playing with design.

Currently, she is interested in exploring the interaction between form and function in her artwork, and expanding her design skills. She is studying Visual Communications and has been playing with apparel graphic design during her free time.
